The Course

The Creative Coding Collective Course is there not only to teach coding but to guide participants towards a career. Maiona Coding is running a series of trainings called the 'Creative Coding Collective' (CCC) in partnership with the Albany in Deptford and funded by the Young Londoners' Fund (Mayor of London).

About Us

The Creative Coding Collective are the first cohort of the Creative Coding Porgram ran by Maiona Coding. We have come together on this project to share what we have learnt so far and also encourage those who are interested in coding and tech to join our coding community, while also giving future participants a platform to express their creativity.
